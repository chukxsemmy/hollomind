Who We Are

Augmenting reality,
amplifying intelligence

Hollomind is a deep-tech spatial computing startup founded in 2024 by MIT and Microsoft computer vision pioneers. We build the infrastructure layer for industrial and enterprise augmented reality, allowing frontline workforces to access AI-driven spatial intelligence in real time.

Our mission is to eliminate cognitive friction on the factory floor, in the warehouse, and across utility networks. By blending advanced machine learning models with spatial hardware, we convert standard enterprise procedures into dynamic, context-aware interactive overlays.
